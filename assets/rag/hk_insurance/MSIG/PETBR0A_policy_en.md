## 1. Policy Introduction

### 1.1 Contract Basis
Your policy is a contract between:
- **Insurer**: MSIG Insurance (Hong Kong) Limited ("We / Us / Our / the Company")
- **Policyholder**: Pet Parent named in the Schedule ("You / Your / Insured / Pet Parent")

The following form one contract package:
- Policy wording
- Schedule
- Any amendment/endorsement
- Application/declaration/information provided at enrollment

### 1.2 Insuring Principle
In return for paid and accepted premium, the Company provides coverage during:
- the stated Period of Insurance, and
- any subsequent accepted renewal period.

### 1.3 Service Commitment
The insurer states that claims should be handled honestly, fairly, and promptly.

## 2. Definitions (Policy-Wide)

### 2.1 Parties and Documents
- **Company / We / Us / Our**: MSIG Insurance (Hong Kong) Limited.
- **You / Your / Insured / Pet Parent**: Policyholder named in Schedule.
- **Schedule**: Document with Pet Parent, Pet details, selected plan, insurance period.
- **Pet**: Domestic cat/dog for companionship/helping use, not commercial use.
- **Your Pet**: Named pet in Schedule.
- **Pet Parent**: Owner of the pet.

### 2.2 Medical and Clinical Terms
- **Accident**: Unexpected and unintended event causing injury.
- **Illness**: Sickness/disease/abnormal health condition.
- **Injury**: Physical harm caused by accident (not illness or gradual wear/tear).
- **Condition**: Clinical manifestations consistent with diagnosis(es), regardless of count/location.
- **Clinical Examination**: Physical exam and comprehensive blood tests (if applicable).
- **Clinical Symptoms**: Detectable anomaly/deviation, including behavioral traits.
- **Diagnostic Tests**: Tests to detect abnormalities/assess pet health.
- **Surgery(ies)**: Invasive operation by veterinarian in operating theater under general anesthesia.
- **Medically Necessary**: Consistent with diagnosis, standard of veterinary practice, not for convenience, and safely appropriate.
- **Supplies**: Veterinarian-determined medically necessary items.

### 2.3 Prevention and Reproductive Terms
- **Vaccination**: Industry-recognized commercial vaccine administered by veterinarian after examination.
- **Dental Health Care**: Routine dental hygiene (e.g., brushing/scaling/polishing/extractions/reconstruction).
- **Neutering**: Surgical removal of both testicles.
- **Spaying**: Ovariohysterectomy/ovariectomy/resection of ovaries and uterus.

### 2.4 Risk and Coverage Terms
- **Commencement Date**: Original inception date shown in Schedule.
- **Period of Insurance**: Policy period shown in Schedule and accepted renewals.
- **Co-insurance**: Portion of claim amount payable by policyholder.
- **Pre-Existing Conditions** include:
  1. Illness/signs/symptoms existing before Commencement Date;
  2. Injury occurring before Commencement Date;
  3. Conditions/complications resulting from pre-commencement illness/injury.

### 2.5 Eligibility/Usage Terms
- **Household**: Family members and others permanently living with you.
- **Hong Kong**: HKSAR, PRC.
- **Racing Dog**: Dog kept for organized race/speed contests.
- **Working Pet**: Pet used in non-companion activities (e.g., racing, breeding, law enforcement, guarding, commercial use).
- **Veterinarian**: Properly licensed/registered practicing vet; excludes policyholder and specified relatives.

## 3. Benefits Structure
All benefits are reimbursement-based for covered conditions, subject to:
- policy terms/conditions,
- Schedule,
- applicable section limits,
- total annual limit.

### 3.1 Section I - Surgical Benefit
1. **Clinical and Surgical Benefit** covers medically necessary surgery expenses, including:
   - Diagnostic tests
   - Veterinarian fee
   - Operating theatre fee
   - Anaesthesia/oxygen fees
   - Surgical implants
   - Related prescribed drugs/injections/dressings/services/supplies
2. **Room and Board Expenses**:
   - Licensed veterinary clinic/hospital confinement
   - Minimum 12 consecutive hours
   - Required for nursing, medication, or monitoring after surgery
3. **Post Surgical Treatment Benefit**:
   - Up to 90 days from surgery date
   - Follow-up consultations, diagnostics, laboratory services, examinations, investigations
   - Includes acupuncture performed by veterinarian
   - Must directly result from surgery-related condition and be recommended by operating veterinarian

### 3.2 Section II - Chemotherapy
Covers chemotherapy costs incurred in licensed veterinary clinic/hospital.

### 3.3 Section III - Final Expenses Benefit
Covers euthanasia and handling of remains, including:
- cremation,
- funeral services,
- handling charges.

Not covered in Section III:
- transport not arranged by veterinarian/funeral provider;
- niche or burial ground costs.

Co-insurance does **not** apply to Section III.

### 3.4 Overall Compensation Limit
Maximum payable for Sections I-III combined cannot exceed **Total Annual Coverage** in Table of Benefits.

### 3.5 Section IV - Third Party Legal Liability
Indemnifies legal liability for accidental:
- bodily injury to third party;
- property loss/damage.

Also covers:
- litigation costs recovered by claimant from insured;
- insured's costs/expenses with insurer's written consent.

Territorial scope: **Hong Kong only**.

Not covered under Section IV:
1. Injury to household members/employees.
2. Damage to property owned/controlled by policyholder, household members, or employees.
3. Deliberate/preventable acts.
4. Fines/penalties/punitive/exemplary expenses.
5. Liability from non-compliance with pet-owner regulations.
6. Certain non-Hong-Kong judgments/enforcement scenarios.

For one claim/series from one event, liability is capped by the Table of Benefits.
If multiple pets are insured, event limit is the highest limit among relevant policies.

## 4. No Claim Discount (NCD)
- 1 preceding claim-free year: **5%**
- 2 consecutive claim-free years: **10%**
- 3+ consecutive claim-free years: **15%**
- Any claim: NCD resets to **0%** at next renewal.

For multiple pets/policies, NCD applies as if each pet has separate policy.
Transferred interest period does not accrue to transferee.

## 5. General Conditions
1. Compliance with policy terms and truthful application are conditions precedent to liability.
2. Unless otherwise agreed in writing, pet must:
   - be micro-chipped;
   - be at least 16 weeks old and below 9 years old at commencement;
   - complete required vaccinations;
   - not be a working pet.
3. Policyholder must be pet owner.
4. Policyholder must take reasonable precautions and comply with legal obligations.
5. Reasonable care duty applies; claim may be denied for breach of care duty.
6. Policy is not transferable to other pets.
7. Coverage applies only while pet is in Hong Kong.
8. Misrepresentation/non-disclosure of material facts may allow alter/amend/cancel/void and forfeit benefits.
9. Insurer may alter policy with at least 30 days written notice; continued premium payment implies acceptance.
10. Premium rates are not guaranteed and may vary for material risk change or portfolio-wide rate increase.
11. Cancellation:
    - By policyholder: written notice; short-period premium retained; refund only if no claim and refund > HK$500.
    - By insurer: 7 days written notice; pro-rata refund.
12. Other insurance/indemnity: compensation on proportionate basis only.
13. No recognition of trust/charge/assignment notice; receipt by insured/legal representative discharges insurer.
14. Hong Kong law and exclusive Hong Kong jurisdiction apply.
15. No third-party enforcement rights under Contracts (Rights of Third Parties) Ordinance (Cap. 623).

## 6. Claims Conditions
1. **Notification**: written notice within 30 days of event.
2. **Proof of claim** includes:
   - completed claim form;
   - enrollment clinical examination result (for first claim);
   - required evidence/documents at insured's expense;
   - written consent to access medical examinations/tests/history;
   - other reasonably required information;
   - original relevant documents and bills required.
3. **Examinations**: insurer may appoint veterinarian to examine pet during claim period.
4. **Arbitration**:
   - disputes on liability/amount handled under Arbitration Ordinance (Cap. 609, Hong Kong);
   - arbitration award is condition precedent to legal action;
   - if not referred within 12 months from disclaimer/rejection, claim is deemed abandoned.

## 7. General Exclusions

### 7.1 Waiting Period / Pre-existing
1. Illness within first 90 days from commencement.
2. Specified hereditary/congenital illness within first 12 months (see Limitations clause 2).
3. Pre-existing conditions.

### 7.2 Preventive / Non-covered Routine
- Spaying/neutering.
- Preventive care (vaccination/titer/flea control/heartworm meds/deworming/nail trim/grooming).
- Parasite control where prophylactic treatments exist.
- Dental health care (except accidental tooth injury extraction/reconstruction).
- Anal gland expression.

### 7.3 Other Non-reimbursable Items (Key List)
Includes, among others:
- Intentional/negligent/preventable acts (including organized dog fighting).
- Elective/cosmetic/preventive procedures (tail docking, ear cropping, de-clawing, micro-chipping, dew claw removal, ear cleaning).
- Transport expenses.
- Hereditary/congenital conditions unless specifically allowed by limitation clause 2.
- Activity-related recurring conditions (before or after insurer warning).
- Contralateral cruciate ligament-related future treatment where pre-existing issue exists.
- Vaccine/prophylaxis-preventable diseases.
- Declared pandemic diseases affecting pet.
- Complications of excluded/limited conditions.
- Detectable pre-commencement abnormalities.
- Claims linked to non-implementation of preventive healthcare.
- Special diets/pet food/vitamins/supplements/grooming-related items.
- Purchase/rental of prostheses/corrective devices/medical appliances.
- Experimental/investigational treatment or medicine.
- Breeding/whelping/queening related conditions.
- Diagnostic tests for excluded/limited conditions.
- Feeding/housing/exercise.
- Behavioural modification/training/therapy/medication.
- Alternative therapies (homeopathy, osteopathy, laser, chiropractic, stem cell therapy, physiotherapy, etc.).
- Extra cost outside usual surgery hours unless emergency criteria met.
- Administrative fees charged by veterinarian (e.g., claim form/report completion).

### 7.4 War and Terrorism Exclusion
Excludes loss/liability/cost directly or indirectly linked to war, terrorism, or related control/prevention actions.

### 7.5 Radioactive / Chemical / Biological Exclusion
Excludes loss/liability/expense related to:
- ionizing radiation/radioactive contamination,
- nuclear facilities/materials,
- atomic/nuclear weapons/devices,
- chemical/biological/biochemical/electromagnetic weapons.

### 7.6 Asbestos Exclusion
Excludes claims/losses arising from asbestos or asbestos-related injury/damage.

### 7.7 Sanctions Limitation
No cover/payment where it would expose (re)insurer to sanctions/prohibitions/restrictions (UN, EU, UK, US, or other applicable regimes).

## 8. Limitations
1. Enrollment clinical examination is mandatory before or within 30 days from commencement; result needed for first claim; failure may void policy.
2. For pets **below 6 years** at commencement: after 12-month waiting period, specified hereditary/congenital conditions may be covered (if not pre-existing):
   - Elbow Dysplasia
   - Luxating Patella
   - Glaucoma
   - Cherry eye
   - Intervertebral Disk Disease (IVDD)
   - Hip Dysplasia (only if specified in Schedule)
3. For pets **6 years or older** at commencement: no hereditary/congenital cover.
4. If not spayed/neutered at commencement: no cover for illnesses related to prostate, hormonal skin conditions, perianal hernias/tumours, testicular tumours, mammary tumours, uterine/ovarian conditions.
