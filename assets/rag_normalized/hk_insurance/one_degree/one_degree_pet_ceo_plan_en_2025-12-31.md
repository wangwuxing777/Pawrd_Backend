# Pet CEO Plan Insurance Policy

> Source: p29
> Unit: policy

## Section A: What You Get From Your Cover

> Source: p33
> Unit: section

### 1. What Your Policy Covers

> Source: p34
> Clause: 1
> Unit: section

#### 1.1 Covered Medical Conditions

> Source: p34-p35
> Clause: 1.1
> Unit: coverage_definition

##### Injuries

> Source: p34
> Clause: 1.1
> Unit: benefit

We will cover medical expenses of any diagnosed injuries caused by Accidents suffered by Your Pet.

We will not cover any injuries caused by Accidents that occur before the end of the Waiting Period.

##### Illness

> Source: p34-p35
> Clause: 1.1
> Unit: benefit

We will cover medical expenses of any diagnosed physical diseases, sicknesses or infections suffered by Your Pet.

We will cover medical expenses for Chronic Medical Conditions subject to the conditions set out in the following paragraphs.

Chronic Medical Conditions:
- Allergies
- Cancer (Malignant)
- Cardiovascular diseases
- Chronic eye diseases
- Chronic hepatobiliary system diseases
- Chronic renal diseases
- Endocrine diseases
- Infectious diseases, including Aujeszky's disease, Feline AIDS, FIV (Feline Immunodeficiency Virus), FeLV (Feline Leukaemia Virus)
- Joint dysplasia
- Osteochondral diseases
- Urinary blockage

##### Chronic Condition Coverage Rule — Pet Age 4 or Below

> Source: p34-p35
> Clause: 1.1
> Unit: eligibility

If Your Pet is 4 years old or below on the later of:
- the first Policy Start Date; or
- the Policy Renewal Date of which You upgrade the Policy to a plan with a higher Annual Limit than the Original Plan;

AND Your Pet has not developed any Symptoms or received a Diagnosis, medication, advice, or treatment for the above Chronic Medical Conditions before the end of the Waiting Period applicable to first policy year and Upgrade of Policy set out in part 2.1;

We will cover the above Chronic Medical Conditions.

##### Chronic Condition Limitation Rule — Pet Age 5 or Above

> Source: p35
> Clause: 1.1
> Unit: eligibility

If Your Pet is 5 years old or above on the earlier of:
- Your first Policy Start Date; or
- the Policy Renewal Date of which You upgrade the Policy to a plan with a higher Annual Limit than the original plan;

We will only provide coverage for the above Chronic Medical Conditions in the policy year that Your Pet first developed Symptoms or received a Diagnosis, medication, advice, or treatment. The related Chronic Medical Conditions will be excluded from the subsequent renewal policies.

In any event, we do not cover any Chronic Medical Conditions for which Your Pet has developed Symptoms or received a Diagnosis, medication, advice, or treatment before the end of the Waiting Period applicable to first Policy Year and Upgrade of Policy set out in part 2.1.

Cross reference: For claims calculation concerning an upgrade of plan, see part 4.6.

#### 1.2 Covered Medical Expenses

> Source: p35-p37
> Clause: 1.2
> Unit: coverage_definition

We will reimburse Your expenses in any covered medical treatments up to the Annual Limit and Reimbursement Rate stated in Your Policy Schedule, provided that:
- A Registered Veterinary Surgeon prescribes and/or performs the treatment, and
- The medical procedure or treatment is deemed Medically Necessary for treating a covered Medical Condition as set out in part 1.1.

##### Surgery

> Source: p36
> Clause: 1.2
> Unit: benefit

We will cover the cost of surgeries and associated surgical expenses, including:
- Surgeon's fees
- Operating theatre's fees
- Anaesthesia fees
- Euthanasia
- Oxygen, dressings, and surgical implants
- Any reasonable and necessary expense for prescribed drugs, injections, dressings and other medical services and supplies related to the surgery

We will not cover consumable items (such as bandages) incurred after the discharge of confinement or during any follow-up treatments.

##### Overnight Hospitalisation

> Source: p36
> Clause: 1.2
> Unit: benefit

We will cover the cost of overnight confinements at clinics and hospitals if Your Pet is hospitalised for a period past midnight.

The cost of hospitalisation on the same day as the treatment that does not continue past midnight will not be covered.

##### Imaging Test

> Source: p36
> Clause: 1.2
> Unit: benefit

We will cover the cost of x-rays and ultrasounds.

We will cover the cost of magnetic resonance imaging (MRI) and computed tomography (CT) if stated in Your Policy Schedule.

Unless stated in Your Policy Schedule, We will not cover any other imaging tests including but not limited to MRI and CT.

##### Laboratory Test

> Source: p36
> Clause: 1.2
> Unit: benefit

We will only cover the following laboratory tests:
- Blood tests
- Urine tests
- Faecal tests
- Biopsy
- Fine needle aspiration cytology

##### Prescribed Medication

> Source: p36
> Clause: 1.2
> Unit: benefit

We will cover the cost of prescribed drugs, dressings and injections dispensed by a Registered Veterinary Surgeon.

##### General Consultation

> Source: p37
> Clause: 1.2
> Unit: benefit

We will cover the consultation fee of a Registered Veterinary Surgeon.

##### Specialist Consultation

> Source: p37
> Clause: 1.2
> Unit: benefit

We will cover the consultation fee of Registered Veterinary Surgeon and emergency consultation.

#### 1.3 Cancer Cash Benefit

> Source: p38
> Clause: 1.3
> Unit: benefit

##### Benefit Trigger

> Source: p38
> Clause: 1.3.1
> Unit: trigger

If Your Pet is diagnosed with cancer for the first time in The Pet's lifetime, We will pay You a one-off cancer cash benefit as stated in Your Policy Schedule.

##### Payout Mechanics

> Source: p38
> Clause: 1.3.2
> Unit: payout

This benefit is a standalone payment to which Your Annual Limit and Reimbursement Rate in Your Policy Schedule do not apply. The payment of this benefit shall not affect the available Annual Limit during that Period of Insurance.

You can only receive this cash benefit once in Your Pet's lifetime. We will not provide any cancer cash pay-outs relating to subsequent cancer occurrences. This applies to Your current or subsequent Period of Insurance specified in any pet insurance plan issued by Us.

##### Eligibility

> Source: p38
> Clause: 1.3.3
> Unit: eligibility

- Your Pet must not develop any symptoms or have received a Diagnosis, medication, advice, or treatment for cancer of any kind before the end of the 180-day Waiting Period set out in part 2.1.
- You will need to submit clear copies of the relevant histopathology reports evidencing the cancer Diagnosis.
- We may ask for further examinations or diagnostic tests. We will pay for these tests.

##### Exclusions

> Source: p38
> Clause: 1.3.4
> Unit: exclusion

This cash benefit is subject to the exclusions listed in part 2 of this Policy, Your Policy Schedule and any other endorsements forming the Policy. We will not provide any cancer cash pay-outs for any cancer related to excluded Medical Conditions.

#### 2.1 Waiting Period

> Source: p43
> Clause: 2.1
> Unit: waiting_period

##### Standard Waiting Period

> Source: p43
> Clause: 2.1.1-2.1.3
> Unit: waiting_period

Your coverage will come into effect after a Waiting Period that runs from the Policy Start Date.

We do not cover any medical expenses incurred during the Waiting Period.

The Waiting Period for all Medical Conditions, except for cancer (malignant), Epileptic Seizures, Quadriplegia, Patellar Luxation (Dislocation), is 28 days.

The Waiting Period for cancer (malignant) is 180 days.

##### MRI/CT Waiting Period

> Source: p43
> Clause: 2.1.4
> Unit: waiting_period

The Waiting Period of covering expenses of magnetic resonance imaging (MRI) and computed tomography (CT) in this Policy is 180 days.

##### Renewal Waiting Period

> Source: p43
> Clause: 2.1.5
> Unit: waiting_period

We will not impose any Waiting Periods for renewals under the same or lower coverage plan.

##### Upgrade Waiting Period

> Source: p43
> Clause: 2.1.6
> Unit: waiting_period

If You upgrade the Policy at renewal where the new coverage plan has a higher Annual Limit than the current coverage plan, the increased portion of Annual Limit shall be subject to a Waiting Period as stated in part 2.1.3.

##### Critical Illness Cash Benefit Waiting Period

> Source: p43
> Clause: 2.1.7
> Unit: waiting_period

If the benefits under part 1.4 were discontinued, cancelled or terminated, Waiting Period for part 1.4 shall be applicable to any future opt-in of the same benefit insuring Your Pet.

##### FIP Additional Coverage Waiting Period

> Source: p43
> Clause: 2.1.8
> Unit: waiting_period

If benefits under part 1.5 is applicable to Your Policy, the Waiting Period for these benefits is 28 days running from the first day of Period of Insurance that includes the feline infectious peritonitis additional coverage. If the benefits under part 1.5 were discontinued, cancelled or terminated, Waiting Period for part 1.5 shall be applicable to any future opt-in of the same benefit insuring Your Pet.

##### Advanced Critical Illness Cash Benefit Waiting Period

> Source: p43
> Clause: 2.1.9

If benefits under part 1.6 is applicable to Your Policy, the Waiting Period for these benefits is 180 days running from the first day of Period of Insurance that includes the advanced critical illness cash benefit. If the benefits under part 1.6 were discontinued, cancelled or terminated, Waiting Period for part 1.6 shall be applicable to any future opt-in of the same benefit insuring Your Pet.

If benefits under part 1.4 is applicable to Your Policy in preceding Period of Insurance and benefits under part 1.6 is applicable to Your Policy in current Period of Insurance, Waiting Period is not applicable to the Medical Conditions which were covered in benefits under part 1.4 in the preceding Period of Insurance.

- Joint dysplasia
- Neoplasia (benign)
- Osteochondral diseases

- Urinary blockage

##### Medical Conditions Caused by Deliberate Harm or Negligence

> Source: p45

##### Medical Conditions Caused by Falling from Height

> Clause: 2.3

### 3. How to Claim

> Source: p47
> Clause: 3
> Unit: section

#### 3.1 Filing a Claim

> Source: p47
> Clause: 3.1
> Unit: claim_rule

You must submit Your claim within 30 days of the consultation date or discharge from the hospital.

To file a claim, log in to Your account on https://www.onedegree.hk/ and fill in the required information. Then You can upload the supporting documents listed on Our website.

#### 3.2 Claim Processing

> Source: p47
> Clause: 3.2
> Unit: claim_rule

The length of time to process a claim depends on the time it takes to receive all the necessary information. We may request more information to assess Your claim, including but not limited to Your Pet's complete medical record, examination, or diagnostic test reports.

We may delay or decline a claim if We are unable to receive the required documents in time.

We will deduct any amount due from You to Us before payment of claims to You.

#### 3.3 Claim Appeal

> Source: p47
> Clause: 3.3
> Unit: claim_rule

If You disagree with a claim decision made by Us, You can contact Us within 60 days from the notice date of the claim decision, providing reasons for the appeal along with supporting documents issued by a Registered Veterinary Surgeon. Otherwise, the claim decision shall be considered final.

#### 3.4 Double Insurance

> Source: p47
> Clause: 3.4
> Unit: claim_rule

If You have successfully reimbursed Your expenses from other insurers, We shall not be liable under this Policy except for the amount not covered by the other insurers, subject to the terms and conditions and Annual Limit of this Policy.

#### 3.5 Private Settlement

> Source: p48
> Clause: 3.5
> Unit: claim_rule

If the Illness or Injury of Your Pet was caused by any act or omission by a third party, and You have successfully been reimbursed all or part of Your expenses from any third party through private settlement, We should not be liable for any expense in relation to such Illness or Injury under this Policy.

#### 3.6 Benefits Received for Claim Event

> Source: p48
> Clause: 3.6
> Unit: claim_rule

We reserve the right to recover any benefits paid to You under a claim, if You have received any payment from any party other than Us in relation to or arising out of the incident, Illness or Injury leading to the said claim.

### 4. Premium and Renewals

> Source: p49
> Clause: 4
> Unit: section

#### 4.1 Period of Insurance

> Source: p49
> Clause: 4.1
> Unit: renewal_rule

The Period of Insurance of this Policy is one calendar year. The start date and end date of the Period of Insurance is stated on the Policy Schedule.

#### 4.2 Billing Options

> Source: p49
> Clause: 4.2
> Unit: renewal_rule

You can choose to pay Your premium monthly or annually.

If You choose to pay a yearly payment on Your Policy Start Date, Your annual renewal premium will be due on the anniversary of the date on which this Policy began.

#### 4.3 Policy Renewal

> Source: p49
> Clause: 4.3
> Unit: renewal_rule

In general, this Policy will be automatically renewed, based on the latest premium and policy terms. We will let You know by email or text message 30 days before the Policy Renewal Date.

We reserve the right to alter premiums or policy terms at renewal.

If You have made or We reasonably suspect You have made a fraudulent claim upon Your application or during any Period of Insurance, We reserve the right not to renew Your Policy at the end of the current Period of Insurance.

#### 4.4 Grace Period

> Source: p49
> Clause: 4.4
> Unit: renewal_rule

There is a grace period of 30 days from the Premium Due Date. If We have not received Your premium when due by the end of the grace period, Your Policy will terminate automatically with retroactive effect from the last Premium Due Date.

#### 4.5 Coverage Change

> Source: p50
> Clause: 4.5
> Unit: renewal_rule

If You wish to make any change to Your Policy, for example, switching to a different coverage plan, You may apply to do so within 30 days prior to the Policy Renewal Date, subject to Our approval.

We do not accept any upgrading the Policy to a plan with a higher Annual Limit if Your Pet will have reached 12 years old at the upcoming renewal.

We reserve the right to alter the premiums and policy terms following a change in coverage plans.

#### 4.6 Coverage Regarding Upgrade and Downgrade of Plan

> Source: p50
> Clause: 4.6
> Unit: renewal_rule

##### Upgrade

> Source: p50
> Clause: 4.6.1
> Unit: renewal_rule

Waiting Period applies to the Additional Coverage due to upgrade of plan. The Additional Coverage will cover Chronic Medical Conditions pursuant to part 1.1, given that Your Pet has not developed any Symptoms or received a Diagnosis, medication, advice, or treatment for the Chronic Medical Conditions stated in part 1.1 before the end of the Waiting Period.

If the upgrade of plan happens on or after Your Pet's turning 5 years old, the age-relevant condition on Chronic Medical Conditions coverage set out in part 1.1 applies to the Additional Coverage but the Original Coverage is unaffected.

##### Downgrade

> Source: p50
> Clause: 4.6.2
> Unit: renewal_rule

In the case of downgrading, there will not be Waiting Period and any Chronic Medical Conditions that is covered in the Original Coverage will not be affected by the downgrade. However, if in the future You wish to upgrade the Policy to a plan with a higher Annual Limit in subsequent Policy Renewal Date(s), coverage to Chronic Medical Conditions is subject to the rules set out in part 4.6.1.

### 5. Cancellation

> Source: p51
> Clause: 5
> Unit: section

#### 5.1 When Does Your Cover End

> Source: p51
> Clause: 5.1
> Unit: renewal_rule

All cover under this Policy will end (whichever is earlier):
- when Your Pet passes away;
- when the Policy is not renewed;
- if the premium is not paid when due after the grace period;
- if the Policy is cancelled by You;
- if the Policy is terminated by Us.

#### 5.2 Your Right to Cancellation

> Source: p51
> Clause: 5.2
> Unit: renewal_rule

You may cancel this Policy if no claim has been paid during the current Period of Insurance.

If You wish to cancel within the first 7 days of the current Period of Insurance, We will refund Your premiums paid for the current Policy in full.

If You choose a yearly payment and wish to cancel after the 7th day of the current Period of Insurance, You will be entitled to a monthly prorated refund after a deduction of 10% cancellation charge on the annual premium.

If You choose a monthly payment and wish to cancel after the 7th day of the current Period of Insurance, no premium refund will be made.

Your Policy coverage ends immediately once We have confirmed the cancellation.

We will not reinstate a cancelled policy.

#### 5.3 Our Right to Terminate

> Source: p52
> Clause: 5.3
> Unit: renewal_rule

We reserve the right to terminate the Policy by providing a 7-day notice by email or text message on the following grounds:
- If there is any misrepresentation, wrong description, or failure to disclose any information other than Age, gender or breed as stated in part 6.5;
- If You make a fraudulent claim;
- If You use threatening, violent, aggressive behaviour or abusive language towards Our staff, contractors, or properties;
- If You have other fraudulent or disruptive behaviour.

No premium will be refunded in any of the cases stated above.
